If there is somethign I have been doing constantly ever since I started working it would have been teaching, I started my teaching career as teaching assistant where I tought pre-grad students some advanced topics like compiler design, and computer graphics as well as teaching intro level topics like Introduction to programming.

Later after I enter the gamedev field, I shifed my effor towards teaching Game development topics for post grad students, and some times to children, I tought for 4 years in a Government packed program targeted towards post grads where I was thier supervisor and helped them get started in the game dev field as well as hellping them with thier project. I later started my company by hiring the best of my students that graduated from this degree.

My teaching career is currently at SAE institute where I teach a bacholor of game development to pre-grads, going back to teach the younger generation was quite the challange at first but it proved to be a much more fulfilling experince, and I only hope my students will be the cornerstone for the gamedev industry in Dubai
